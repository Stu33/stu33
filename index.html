The instructions say to download a starter file, but don't bother. Instead:

  • Go to the finished example project and click the "See Inside" button.
  • Delete the Cat Flyin, Crystal and Game Over sprites.
  • Select the Stars sprite and delete all of its code. Then click the eye with a line through it icon (hide) for the "Show" setting.
  • Now follow the rest of the instructions to make your own game. When you're told to upload stars.png for the Stars sprite, just unhide the one you hid instead.
